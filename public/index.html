Common Questions
What is an IP address?
It's like a mailing address for your internet connection. It helps other computers find yours so websites can send you information.
Is it safe to share?
Yes! Sharing your IP address with a support person is perfectly normal and safe. It helps them troubleshoot your connection.
What's IPv4 vs IPv6?
They're just two formats for IP addresses. IPv4 looks like 192.168.1.42 (numbers and dots). IPv6 is a newer, longer format like 2001:db8::1. Most support teams just need your IPv4 address, but you can find your IPv6 below your IPv4 address above if needed.
